Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 350g Spaghetti
  • 150g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs (M)
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 125g Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 125g Pecorino Romano
  • Some Olive Oil
  • Some Pasta Water
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper (freshly grated)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, salt heavily and cook the pasta until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, crack two eggs into a large bowl and separate the yolks of the other two eggs. Put the two yolks into the bowl and discard the egg whites.
  3. Grate parmigiano and pecorino into the bowl and season with black pepper and some salt. The Mixture should be doughy in texture, not too liquidy.
  4. Cut the pancetta in cubes and heat olive oil in a pan, fry the pancteta until it is crispy.
  5. Remove the pancetta from the pan and put most into the bowl as well. Keep some of the fried pancetta as a topping.
  6. When the pasta is al dente, keep some pasta water in a mug, afterwards drain the pasta.
  7. Stir the drained pasta in the hot olive oil, then add it to the large bowl as well.
  8. Gently mix the pasta and the egg-and-cheese paste until the pasta is completely coated in a creamy sauce.
  9. If the consistency is too thick, (sparingly) add some pasta water until you get a creamy texture.
  10. Top off with fried pancetta cubes and enjoy while it's still hot!
