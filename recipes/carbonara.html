Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 350g Spaghetti
  • 150g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs (M)
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 125g Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 125g Pecorino Romano
  • Some Olive Oil
  • Some Pasta Water
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper (freshly grated)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, salt heavily and put in the pasta until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, crack two eggs into a large bowl and separate the yolks of the other two eggs. Put them also in the bowl.
  3. Grate the parmigiano and pecorino into the same bowl and season with black pepper and some salt. The Mixture should be doughy in texture, not too liquidy.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the pancteta until it is crispy.
  5. Remove the pancetta from the pan and put it into the bowl with the egg and cheese paste.
  6. When the pasta is al dente, keep some pasta water in a mug, afterwards drain it.
  7. Stir the drained pasta in the hot olive oil, then add them to the large bowl as well.
  8. Gently mix pasta and the egg and cheese paste until the pasta is completely coated in a creamy sauce.
  9. If the consistency is too thick, add some pasta water sparingly until you get a creamy texture.
  10. Enjoy while it's still hot!
