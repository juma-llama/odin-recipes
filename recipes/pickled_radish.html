Pickled Radish

Ingredients

  • 1kg Radish
  • 250g Cucumber
  • 750ml Vinegar
  • 750ml Water
  • 5 Tablespoons Salt
  • 10 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 3 Teaspoons Mustard Seeds
  • 24 Peppercorns (mixed)
  • Some Dill (optional)
  • Clean Glass Jars

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the radish and cucumber into whichever form you like.
  2. Wash and cut the dill.
  3. Fill your clean glass jars with radish, cucumber and some dill until it is filled to about 3/4.
  4. Meanwhile, bring all the other ingredients to a boil.
  5. Once sugar and salt are fully dissolved, fill the glass jars with the (lightly boiling) fluid to the brim.
  6. Close the lid and carefully turn the jar upside down. If you can see any bubbles entering the jar, you need to fasten the lid more.
  7. Be careful while handling the hot jars and use oven and use oven gloves to prevent burning yourself!
  8. Let the jars cool down and check, whether a vacuum seal formed.
  9. If all the jars are properly sealed, store them in a cool and dark place for 14 days.
  10. After 14 days you can enjoy your pickled radish!
  11. Your pickles will preserve for about 3 months. We recommend storing opened jars in the fridge, where they will stay fresh for about 3 weeks.
