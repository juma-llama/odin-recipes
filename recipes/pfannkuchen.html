Pfannkuchen

Ingredients

  • 400g Flour
  • 750ml Milk (low fat)
  • 3 Eggs (M)
  • 1 Tablespoon Salt
  • Some Mineral Water
  • Some Butter (for the frying pan)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, eggs, and salt until you have a smooth, slightly runny dough.
  2. Set aside and cool for half an hour.
  3. Add the mineral water and gently stir once or twice.
  4. Add butter to the heated frying pan.
  5. Add the dough and wait until the dough is crispy and golden on one side, then flip to the other side and bake until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Enjoy with some fruit or chocolate cream or with cheese and ham for a savoury experience.
